In this experiment, you might encounter 4 environments:

The price can be either fixed or flexible:
If price is flexible: sellers are allow to enter the price within a given range at Stage 2.
If price is fixed: seller still need to input their price at Stage 2 but there will be only one option.
The seller's market entry can be either simultaneous or sequential:
If market entry is simultaneous: all sellers are in the experiment from round 1 and can choose whether to enter the market, the price and the product.
If market entry is sequential: Half of the sellers play the experiment as usual. The other half cannot enter the market at the beginning of the experiment and can join at round 5. If you are randomly selected to join the experiment at round {{ Constants.mid_round }}, you cannot make any decision before {{ Constants.mid_round }} and will receive {{ Constants.e_s }} every round.
